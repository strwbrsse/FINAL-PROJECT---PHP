About Us

Welcome to ShotSafe!

ShotSafe is a simple, secure, and user-friendly vaccination management system designed to help individuals and healthcare providers easily manage vaccination records. Our goal is to create an efficient way to keep track of vaccinations, ensuring safety and ease of access for everyone involved.

What We Do

ShotSafe allows users to:

  • Securely store vaccination records.
  • Easily track upcoming vaccines and appointments.
  • Ensure privacy and data protection for all users.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a reliable platform that empowers individuals and healthcare providers to effectively manage vaccination schedules, ensure public health safety, and reduce the risks of vaccine-preventable diseases. We aim to simplify the vaccination process and make it more accessible for everyone.

Our Team

This project was created by a group of students from Batangas State University, with the goal of combining technology with healthcare to address real-world challenges in vaccine management. Our team is dedicated to providing the best possible experience for users and contributing to the global fight against vaccine-preventable diseases.

Future Plans

We are constantly improving ShotSafe to provide more features and enhance the user experience. Some of our future plans include:

  • Integration with health insurance providers for easier vaccination claim processing.
  • Adding support for additional languages to cater to a wider audience.
  • Expanding our platform to include international vaccination tracking and certifications.
